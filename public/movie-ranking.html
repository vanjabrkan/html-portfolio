The Best Movies According to Vanja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lord of the Rings

This is my favourite movie. The trilogy follows the journey of Frodo Baggins, a young hobbit from the Shire, who inherits a powerful but dangerous ring from his uncle, Bilbo Baggins. This ring, forged by the dark lord Sauron, holds immense power and threatens to corrupt and control all of Middle-earth if it falls into the wrong hands. With the help of a diverse fellowship consisting of hobbits, elves, dwarves, men, and wizards, Frodo sets out on a perilous quest to destroy the ring in the fires of Mount Doom, where it was forged.

Harry Potter

The story begins with "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone"), where we meet Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday. He receives an acceptance letter to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and begins his journey into the magical world.

Room

The story revolves around Jack, a bright and curious five-year-old boy, and his loving mother Ma, who are held captive in a small room. The room is the only world Jack has ever known, and he believes it to be the entirety of the universe. Ma, however, has created a nurturing environment for Jack despite their harrowing circumstances, shielding him from the grim reality of their captivity as much as possible.
